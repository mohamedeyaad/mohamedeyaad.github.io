About Me

Robotics and Machine Learning Engineer

I am a mechatronics engineer (class of 2020) with hands-on experience in machine learning and robotics. Passionate about increasing the cognitive abilities of machines and robots, making their interaction with their environment smoother and smarter. Always seeking opportunities to contribute and improve my skills by working on projects and conducting research in robotics and machine learning.

My first day in college was like a dream to me. I daydreamed all the day of the many things I will do in this place and about that last day in graduation gown celebrating the countless achievements I will have achieved by then. My last day in college was a bit different than I imagined; Graduating in a global pandemic was by no means a thing that I would have imagined. I, Nevertheless, was celebrating all I wanted and dreamed of; Two scholarships, one research grant, a member of many teams, a team leader, honoured by the president (in a team), top 1% on my class, and a published paper.

I am currently a robotics software engineer at Kingdom technologies Ltd, Glasgow, UK.

What do I do now?

I am currently working as a robotics software engineer at Kingdom Technologies Ltd. My day-to-daytasks are about developing and deploying new software features for the current running robots, maintaining and updating the current running software, and researching and planing for future features and improvements.
My job description focuses more on perception tasks. But coming from a mechatronics background, I usually do a task here and there outside of the software boundaries, especially when it comes to integration with other departments.

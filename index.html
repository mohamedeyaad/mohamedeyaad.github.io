About me

Senior Undergraduate Mechatronics Engineer

I am a Senior undergraduate Mechatronics Engineer at Ain Shams University. Currently working on my Bachelor graduation project which is about the development of a Collaborative Assembly Serial-Manipulator using Reinforcement Learning. I was born in Cairo, Egypt. My interest in engineering started in childhood. I was always amazed to see robots in TV shows, cars on highways, and simple appliances at home. I am always passionate about increasing the cognitive abilities of machines and robots, making their interaction with their environment smoother and smarter. Always seeking opportunities to contribute and improve my skills by working on projects and conducting research in robotics and machine learning.

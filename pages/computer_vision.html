Vehicle Counting, Classification & Detection using YOLOv5

This project was part of the "Computer Vision" course @ Faculty of Engineering - Ain Shams University, Egypt. Applied computer vision techniques to classify vehicles on the road into 2 classes: HMV(heavy motor vehicle) or LMV( light motor vehicle) and also count the number of vehicles that travel through a road.

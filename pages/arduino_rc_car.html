Arduino RC Car

This project was part of a robotics competition "ihub" held by Faculty of Engineering - Ain Shams University, Egypt. The main target of the project is to design and build RC robotic car controlled by Arduino and compete with other competitiors.

This video shows the robot on the challenging track made by ihub, we took the shortest time among other competitiors to finish this track.

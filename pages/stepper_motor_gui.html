Stepper Motor GUI

This is a Project was part of the "Electromechanical Systems" Course @ UCLan (University of Central Lanchasire) in Preston, UK. It was during my exchange program which is funded and supported by Erasmums+. UCLan and Erasmus representitives tested and interviewed the top students of Ain Shams University, me and other 3 colleauges were selected from our university.

The aim of the project is to apply the concepts of electromechanical systems to control the speed and the direction of 2 steppper motors using a GUI (Graphical User Interface). I have used LabVIEW software to design the GUI and interfaced it with Arduino to give signals for the motor driver to control the motors.
